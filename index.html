🗺️ How PediAid Fits Together

👩‍⚕️
Pediatricians & users
Doctors open PediAid on their phone, the web, or through the app stores.
▼they use one of these front doors
📱
PediAid App
pediaid.bridgr.co.in
The main app — calculators, growth charts, drug formulary, guides. Same code runs on Web, Android & iOS (Flutter).
🎓
PediAid Academics
academics.pediaid.bridgr.co.in
Guides, chapters, CME events & trial summaries. Opens inside the app too (the Academics tab).
📄
Landing Page
info.pediaid.bridgr.co.in
Public marketing page & privacy policy — what new users see first.
▼all of them talk to one brain
⚙️
Backend API — Render
pediaid-backend.onrender.com
Handles logins, guides & chapters, the visitor counter, "report an issue" emails, and your push-notification broadcasts. Auto-deploys whenever code is pushed to GitHub.
▼which stores & sends through
🗄️
Neon PostgreSQL
The database — users, guides, CME records, visit counts. Singapore region, free tier.
🔔
Firebase Cloud Messaging
Google's push-notification service. The panel above sends through it to every subscribed device.
✉️
Gmail SMTP
Sends emails — password resets, and every "report an issue" tap lands in your inbox.
